Volunteer

Volunteers Welcome!

Volunteers come for many reasons: to serve the community, to learn new skills, to meet new people, to enrich their cultural understanding, to breathe fresh air, to commune with nature, and to enjoy beautiful views and a peaceful environment.

Why Volunteer

Sakyadhita Hawai’i encourages an economy of compassion. Volunteers come to La’i Peace Center to serve the community, learn new skills, meet new people, enrich their cultural awareness, breathe fresh air, and appreciate nature, all while enjoying beautiful views and a peaceful environment!
